# No AI Slop — for Google Ads copy

Adapted from the stop-slop rules for short-form ad copy. The goal: ads that sound like a sharp human marketer wrote them, not a model. Apply to headlines, long headlines, and descriptions.

## Banned words (the classic AI ad tells)

These scream "AI wrote this." Never use them:

- Unlock, Unleash
- Elevate, Elevated
- Discover, Discover the
- Seamless, Seamlessly
- Effortless, Effortlessly
- Empower, Empowering
- Revolutionize, Revolutionary
- Game-changer, Game-changing
- Cutting-edge, State-of-the-art, Best-in-class, World-class
- Transform your, Transformative
- Supercharge, Level up, Take it to the next level
- Dive in, Dive into
- Embark on a journey, Your journey
- Experience the difference
- Look no further
- Say goodbye to [X]
- Your one-stop shop
- We've got you covered
- Tailored to your needs, Tailored just for you
- Solutions that [verb]
- In today's world / In today's fast-paced world
- The future of [X]
- Reimagine, Redefine

## Banned phrases / openers

- "Here's what..." / "Here's why..." / "Here's the thing"
- "It's worth noting"
- "When it comes to [X]"
- "At the end of the day"
- "The reality is"
- "More than just [X]"
- "Not just [X], but [Y]" (the false-contrast formula)

## Adverbs — cut all -ly words

really, just, simply, truly, genuinely, honestly, actually, effortlessly, seamlessly, instantly (unless literally true and provable), perfectly, easily (prefer "Easy [X]").

## Punctuation

- No em dashes (—). Use shorter lines or a period.
- No exclamation overload. One "!" max per ad, and only if it earns it.

## Replace vague with specific

| AI vague | Human specific |
|----------|----------------|
| Premium quality | Solid oak, hand-finished |
| Great support | 24/7 phone support |
| Fast delivery | Same-day delivery |
| Affordable prices | From $19/mo |
| Trusted by many | Trusted by 4,000+ shops |
| Expert team | 15 yrs in business |
| Wide selection | 500+ models in stock |
| Satisfaction guaranteed | 30-day money-back |

Only use the specific version if the user gave you that fact. Never invent the number.

## Strong CTA verbs (use these, not generic AI CTAs)

Order Now, Book Today, Call Us, Get a Quote, Shop the Sale, Start Free, Try It Free, Schedule a Visit, Claim Your Offer, See Pricing, Find Your Size, Reserve a Spot.

Avoid AI-default CTAs like "Discover more", "Learn more about", "Explore our", "Unlock your".

## The test

Read the line out loud. If it sounds like every other ad on the internet, rewrite it with a real fact, a real number, or a real verb. Specific beats clever.
